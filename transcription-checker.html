Welcome to the Modern Transcription Checker!

  • Step 1: Paste the original dictation text into the "Original Passage" box.
  • Step 2: Type or paste your transcribed text into the "Your Transcription" box.
  • Step 3: Click "Check Accuracy" to see a detailed, interactive report.
  • Timer Mode: Pasting into the top box starts an optional 50-minute typing timer.
  • Re-evaluate: On the results page, you can edit the original text and re-check your score.
